Quick Tips

While making Ladi Pav or Bread or bun knead the dough for almost 10 minutes. Also the dough must be very sticky
Heat the oil thoroughly before adding seasonings or during deep frying
Adding Butter to Ladi Pav dough makes it soft and spongy, Don't forget to grease bun with butter after taking out from Oven to get beautiful brown color
